# How to Join Us

Jan works because people contribute. The more people who use it, improve it, and share it, the better it gets.

## Join the community

Start in our [Discord](https://discord.gg/FTk2MvZwJH). That's where everything happens. You can:

* Join discussions and follow what we are building
* Share ideas, feedback, and experiments
* Learn from others and help newcomers find their way

## Contribute

Jan is [open source](https://github.com/janhq). You can:  

* Write or review code
* Report bugs or give feedback
