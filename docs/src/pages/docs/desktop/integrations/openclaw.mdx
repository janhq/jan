# OpenClaw

OpenClaw is an autonomous AI agent that runs on your own hardware. Unlike a chatbot, it takes real actions on your behalf — sending messages, managing files, and automating tasks across your connected channels.

![OpenClaw Integration](../_assets/openclaw-integration.png)

## Channels

Once enabled, OpenClaw can send and receive messages through connected channels. The screenshot above shows Telegram and WhatsApp both connected and active.

Supported channels:
- **Telegram**
- **WhatsApp**
- Discord
- Slack

## Setup

<Steps>

### Open Settings

Go to **Settings** → **Integrations** → **OpenClaw**.

### Enable OpenClaw

Click **Start** next to **Enable OpenClaw**.

### Connect channels

Add your messaging channels — Telegram, WhatsApp, or others. Each channel has a built-in tutorial walkthrough inside the app that guides you through every step of the connection process. Follow the in-app steps to authenticate and connect. Once active, the channel will show a green **Connected** status.

</Steps>

## What OpenClaw can do

- Read and manage local files on your computer
- Manage your calendar
- Send and receive messages via connected channels
- Execute commands and automate tasks

**Skills:** Extend OpenClaw with skills to add new capabilities and integrations.
